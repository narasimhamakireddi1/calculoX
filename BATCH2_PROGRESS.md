## 📊 Expansion Template (Proven Pattern)

Each calculator guide post should follow this structure:

### Section 1: The Challenge / Problem Statement
- User's pain point
- Why this matters financially
- Cost of not understanding (e.g., "₹50L loan costs ₹48.75L extra in interest")
- What this guide solves
- **Target:** 250-300 words

### Section 2: What is X? (Definition)
- Core concept explained
- Why it matters
- Real-world context
- Common misconceptions addressed
- **Target:** 200-250 words

### Section 3: How to Calculate X? (Formula + Worked Example)
- Formula presentation
- Step-by-step worked example
- Real numbers (Indian context)
- Key insights about formula
- **Target:** 250-300 words

### Section 4: X in Real Life (Examples/Use Cases)
- 3-5 practical examples
- Indian financial scenarios
- Diverse income levels
- **Target:** 300-400 words

- 3 different advanced sections
- Practical actionable tips
- Quantified benefits
- Real-world success stories
- **Target:** 250-300 words each

### Section 8: Calculator Usage & Implementation
- How to use the free calculator
- Common scenarios to test
- Decision-making framework
- **Target:** 200-250 words

### FAQs (6-8 minimum)
- Each answer 100-150+ words
- Real questions people ask
- Actionable advice
- **Target:** 7-8 FAQs total
